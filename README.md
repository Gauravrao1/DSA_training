# 🔢 Number System Problems

## 1. Prime Number Check
Check whether a number is prime.

**Example**
Input: 29  
Output: True

**Concept:** A prime number has only two factors — 1 and itself.

---

## 2. Pythagorean Triplets
Generate all triplets where:

a² + b² = c²

**Input:** limit = 20  
**Output:**
3 4 5
6 8 10
5 12 13
8 15 17
12 16 20

---

## 3. Maximum Marks Per Semester
Find maximum marks scored in each semester.

**Rule:**  
Marks must be between **0 and 100**  
Otherwise print: `"You have entered invalid mark"`

---

## 4. Algebraic Expression
Solve:

a³ + a²b + 2a²b + 2ab² + ab² + b³

**Simplified Result:**

(a + b)³

---

## 5. Tyres in Dealerships
Calculate total tyres based on vehicles.

**Formula:**
Tyres = (Cars × 4) + (Bikes × 2)


---

## 6. Minimum Discount Item
Find the product with the **minimum discount amount**.

**Formula:**

Discount = Price × Discount%


---

## 7. Factors of a Number
Print all factors of a number.

**Rules:**
- Negative → Ignore sign
- Zero → "No Factors"

**Example:**  
Input: 54  
Output: 1,2,3,6,9,18,27,54
